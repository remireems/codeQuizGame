Quiz Rules
  1. You will only have 60 seconds to finish the quiz.
  2. You only get one try on each question.
  3. Every time you get a question wrong, you will lose 10 seconds on the timer.
  4. When you run out of time, the quiz will end.
  5. Every correct answer will give you a point!
  6. Once you have finished the quiz, you can record your highscore.
  7. Good luck on the quiz and try to get the highest score!
